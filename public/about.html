About Me

I am a father, musician, canoeist, and traveler. I love movies, concerts, walking in the woods, and visiting friends and family near and far.

I play baritone saxiphone in a big band called Mood Swing. Our next performance is a dinner and dance at Club Lorelei, in town. I also play guitar at campfires and family get-to-gethers. My father (who is 88 years young) plays 4-string banjo, and fiddle. Other family members play guitar, drums, keyboards, and sing as well. YeeHa!

I recently bought an new (ish) car - a Rav4. I'm a Highway Star!
